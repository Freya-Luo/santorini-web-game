## Santorini

#### **How to Start and Details**
1. Click the bottom button `start game` and type in two different names of players, and then click `OK`
2. Then click `choose god` button and select two different gods, and click `OK`
3. Now, randomly select the cell for picking up the starting positions for your workers.
   1. the playerA's workers are labeled as `X`
   2. the playerB's workers are labeled as `O`
4. The instruction popped up in yellow shows which player should act in this turn
5. Invalid action will be notified by the popped red messages at the top
6. The towers' heights are indicated by the different colors. Detailed labels are shown at the top of the game board
7. The button at the bottom indicates the next step and shows the game status (mostly showing `running`). 
8. When one of the players wins, the button will be reset to `start game`. Click it and retype players' names, a new game will start.
